Projects

We are dedicated and passionate about helping women and girls in underserved communities. These are some of our projects.



10 Days Menstrual Hygiene Advocacy, 2021.

We had the period talk with school girls, sensitized them on need for hygiene and distributed sanitary pads. While women attend to school girls while guys educated school boys on how they can help with as little as fetching a bucket of water.

International Women's Day 2022.

The overview of our Girl Code Bootcamp, which intended to #breakthebias by training 20 young girls with tech skills and contributing to the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). 1 – No Poverty, 4 – Quality Education, 8 – Decent Work and Economic Growth, 10 – Reduced Inequalities, and 17 – Partnerships for the International Women's Day 2022 Goals.

Menstrual Hygiene Day, 2022

Period poverty encompasses not just a lack of access to period supplies, but also a lack of knowledge about menstrual hygiene. SAFWGS is committed to filling in the gaps in menstrual hygiene education, eradicating the stigma associated with menstruation, and continuing to raise awareness about the barriers to menstrual product access.
